About The English Tree

Two decades of excellence in English language training

Established in 2003, The English Tree Training Institute has evolved into a renowned and professional brand, delivering unparalleled corporate training services to clients both locally and nationally. As a 100% service-oriented institute, we are driven by our team's genuine passion for training and development programs.

At The English Tree, we are committed to providing exceptional training excellence to our clients. Our core team consists of highly talented corporate trainers who are proficient in meeting the high expectations of our clients.

All trainers at our centre are highly qualified and certified by the British Council, including a PhD scholar in English Language Education and a Celta-certified trainer from Cambridge. Each team member possesses long-term teaching experience and demonstrates a deep commitment and passion for teaching.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with exceptional English language skills through innovative teaching methodologies, personalized attention, and a commitment to excellence that transforms lives and careers.
